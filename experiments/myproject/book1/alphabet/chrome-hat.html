"Chrome Hat"

Alphabet Hero

Teleport-Teleport Fruit User (Speculated)

"Chrome Hat" is an unknown man that are able to teleport stylishly by touching his eponymous hat, speculated to be granted by a power fruit, the Teleport-Teleport Fruit. He is also suspected to belong in a group of powered people with unknown intentions.

Abilities

Multi-Punch

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

"Chrome Hat" execute the combo: Punch. Teleport. Punch. Teleport. Punch. Teleport. Punch.

Counter Stunner

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

"Chrome Hat" stuns any enemy who attacks with melee attacks. 100% Counter Chance, 100% Stun Chance.

Chromization

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

"Chrome Hat" turns into a chrome version of himself, and gets two turns starting next turn.

Flying Teleport Combo

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

"Chrome Hat" punch an enemy to the air and teleport punches repeatedly, keeping enemy in the air. Inflicts Falling.
