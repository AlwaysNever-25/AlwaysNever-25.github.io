J

Alphabet Hero

Project X: Slo-Mo Potion, "The Cane Sword"

"J" is a swordsman who owns "The Cane Sword". He is a member of Project X after he drank an imperfect potion of Slow Motion. He is also known as "The Beast with a Weird Nose" amongst the members of the 100 Good Swordsmen, because of his j-shaped nose that earns him his name.

Abilities

Slow Slash

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

J slows the time around an enemy and slash them, delaying them for one turn. At the start of their next turn, his slash activates, dealing damage.

Take It Slow

(Cooldown: 2 Turns, Aura)

J slows the area around him, making it harder for enemies to hit him, but he is unable to attack normally for the duration (skills only).

Requiem of J

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

J stabs the enemy ten times.

Humming Attack

(Cooldown: 4 Turns)

J hits enemies quickly for massive damage, but the attack is delayed for three turns, or until the enemy does an attack.
