Baron "Hoodie" Bored

Alphabet Hero

Dodge-Dodge Fruit User

Baron "Hoodie" Bored is a hobo turned professional burglar that awakened his sense of slipperiness after eating the Dodge-Dodge Power Fruit, a feat of its own due to the slippery nature of the fruit itself. Due to the powers, he is impervious to ranged attack. The fruit allows him to sense danger and overclock his reflex, almost to a superhuman level, but he is still weak to a sure-hit attack.

Abilities

Counterjab

(Passive)

Baron counters any unarmed attack. 75% Chance to Counter-Attack

Backstab

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Baron moves quickly to the enemy's back and stabs them. 25% Insta-kill chance on a non-boss and non-elite enemy. If Insta-kill is proc'd, Cooldown is raised to three turns.

Bored

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

Baron gets in the zone, and able to counter any attacks (including melee and ranged) for two turns.

Sweep

(Cooldown: 4 Turns, AoE)

Baron sweeps the leg of everyone smoothly, crippling them. 100% chance to Cripple enemies.

Innate Abilities

"Hoodie

(Hidden, Passive)

Baron is impervious to any ranged attacks.
