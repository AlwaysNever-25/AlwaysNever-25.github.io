Wright "Wicked" Wilkinson

Alphabet Hero

Seven Great Swordsmen (The Invisible Twin)

Wright "Wicked" Wilkinson is the No. 5 swordsman in the world, wielding one of the 7 Great Swords, "The Invisible Twin". The sword is famously invisible and cannot be perceived by any means, except if its owner allow you to see it.

Abilities

Invisible Slash

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Wright "Wicked" Wilkinson catch an enemy by surprise, hitting an enemy and crippling them.

Invisible Parry

(Passive)

Wright "Wicked" Wilkinson uses his invisible swords to parry attacks that comes close to him. 100% Chance to Parry Melee Attacks, but the chance decreases the more Wright uses Invisible Slash and Wickedness in the World, with each use dropping the chances by 10%, capping at 30% chance.

The Wicked Glance

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Wright "Wicked" Wilkinson's wild eyes struck fear in enemies. 75% chance to inflict Fear debuff.

Wickedness in the World

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

Wright "Wicked" Wilkinson slash wickedly at multiple enemies, dealing massive bleed damage.

Innate Abilities

The Wicked Does Not Believe In The Creator

(Hidden, Passive)

When Wright "Wickedness" Wilkinson fight against enemies that represented The Creator, his damage is buffed by 50%.
