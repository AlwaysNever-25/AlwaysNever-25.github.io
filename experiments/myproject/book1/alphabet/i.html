I

Alphabet Hero

Paper-Paper Fruit User

"I" (or Mr. i) is a wrestler that accidentally granted the power to turn himself into paper version of himself, with unparalleled control.

Prev:

Harry "Hunter" Jackson

Next:

j, the "Cane Sword" Swordsman

Abilities (Non-Paper Fruit Mode)

Bashing Lariat

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

I bashes an enemy and strike others with your arms to their necks.

Protector

(Cooldown: 2 Turns, Aura)

I guard a teammate, reducing the damage they take.

Paper Mode

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

I turn himself into paper mode, making himself harder to get hit, but lowers his health significantly.

Cannonball

(Cooldown: 4 Turns)

I turns into semi-paper mode and curl himself into a heavy ball, smashing an enemy dealing heavy damage.

Abilities (Paper Fruit Mode)

Paper Shuriken

(Cooldown: No Cooldown)

I torn part of himself to turn into shuriken, a sharp knife-like object, that he threw at an enemy. I's health gets slightly lowered each time he uses this skill.

Paper Armor

(Cooldown: 2 Turns, Aura)

I turn himself into an armor made of strengthened paper and enveloping a teammate, reducing the damage they take for a specific amount.

Paper Off

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

I turn himself back to normal, making himself easier to get hit, but raises his health significantly.

Paper Cuts

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

I turns his arms into sharp edges and harries enemies, dealing paper cuts and bleeding enemies.
