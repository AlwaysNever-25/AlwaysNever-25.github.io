Elena "El-ena" Eloski

Alphabet Hero

Project X Members (Electric Shock Therapy)

Elena "El-ena" Eloski is a prisoner arrested because of a false drug possession. She got her electric powers because of the energy ripples that Master Knife emmited during his attack that coincides with her electric shock therapy that she receives. She now sows revenge for the police officers that framed her, using her power to find them.

Abilities

Handshake

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Elena "El-ena" Eloski closes up to the enemy and give them a handshake, with added electricity, of course.

Bolt of Lightning

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Elena "El-ena" Eloski strikes an enemy with a ranged lightning bolt, stunning them for 2 turns. 100% Stunning Chance

El-ectrify

(Cooldown: 2 Turns, Aura)

Elena "El-ena" Eloski strikes down anyone who attacks her with unarmed or melee weapons containing metal in it with electricity, shocking them and stunning them for 1 turn.

Electric Tornado

(Cooldown: 4 Turns, AoE)

Elena "El-ena" Eloski creates a tornado charged with lightning, striking lightning bolts towards enemies.
