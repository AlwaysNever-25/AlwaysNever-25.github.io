"Gears"

Alphabet Hero

Gears-Gears Fruit User (Speculated)

"Gears" is an unknown vigilante that speculated to have come across the Gears-Gears Fruit. Based on their familiarity with the gears and how to best use them, it is theorized that this person might be a mechanic or formerly employed as one.

  2. Wrench
  3. Cogs

Feet

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

"Gears" turns his feet into cogs to help him in battle.

  1. Cogfeet Dexterity
  2. Cogfeet Attack

Spring Attack

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

"Gears" turn his feet into springs and jumps off, then strike enemy from above with his hammer.

Spring Punches

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

"Gears" punch enemies with hammer hand enhanced with spring arms, dealing massive AoE damage.

Innate Abilities

Cramped Space

(Passive)
"Gears" has a specific skill that can only be used if the battle stage is indoor, replacing "Spring Punches" with "Spring Combos".

Spring Combos

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)
"Gears" turn his entire body into springs, bouncing around the wall and hitting all enemies until he reached the critical momentum. He then hit a single enemy, dealing massive damage.
