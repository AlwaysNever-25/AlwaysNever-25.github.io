Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau

Alphabet Hero

Seven Great Swordsmen (The Infinity)

Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau is the No. 1 swordsman in the world, wielding one of the 7 Great Swords, "The Infinity". Ned found the sword in his backyard after a mysterious meteor shower hit his city, the byproduct of Master Knife's vicious rampage. He is rumoured to have killed 1000 challengers for his title, all of them former member of the 100 Good Swordsmen. It is also rumoured that he has direct contact with The Creator through his sword, as the sword is one of The Creator's Gifts.

Abilities

Sound of Nothing

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau slashes the enemy without making a sound. Inflicts an enemy with the debuff "Wounds: Sound of Nothing".

It's Nothing

(Passive)

Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau are granted superhuman level of endurance. The damage of the first attack that hits him gets halved, but subsequent attack's damage gets doubled.

Infinity Slash

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau slashes an enemy multiple times, dealing heavy damage. Enemies inflicted by the debuff "Wounds: Sound of Nothing" will explode outward, dealing damage to all enemies and remove the debuff. If there's other enemies beside them that also have the debuff "Wounds: Sound of Nothing", they also explode outward and deals damage to all enemies.

Hailstorm of Infinity

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau becomes the meteor shower that he sees before he got his swords, dealing hailstorm of sword slashes towards enemies.

Innate Abilities

Infinity

(Hidden, Passive)

Due to "The Infinity" influence, Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau has two tiers of HP, and has a relatively high defense. Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau enters a trance after one tier of his HP is depleted, where he traded his high defense for an overwhelmingly high attack. The passive "It's Nothing" is replaced by "Nothing Matters".

Nothing Matters

(Passive)

Ned "Nothingness" Neverbrau became reckless with his defense, but still measured in his attacks. Attacks that damaged him are now always doubled, but his attack's damage toward others are quadrupled.
