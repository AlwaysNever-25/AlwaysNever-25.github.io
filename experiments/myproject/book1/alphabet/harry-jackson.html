Project X: Sniper Program

Harry "Hunter" Jackson is a trained sniper from Project X, and one of their best agent. Some even claim that he is one of the best snipers in the world. Due to his active association with the project, other details about him are mostly classified.

Abilities

Your Forehead's Too Big

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Harry "Hunter" Jackson hit the enemy right in their forehead with his slingshot, staggering them.

Hawkeye

(Passive)

Harry "Hunter" Jackson never misses. Most of the time. Fixed 90% chance to hit his ranged attack, and cannot be debuffed by any means.

I Purposefully Miss That Shot By The Way

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

Harry "Hunter" Jackson hit a structure above the enemy, dropping them toward an enemy and stunning them.

I Hate... That Guy!

(Cooldown: 4 Turns, Mark)

Harry "Hunter" Jackson targets a specific enemy and grants anyone who attacks them two turns specifically to attack them.
