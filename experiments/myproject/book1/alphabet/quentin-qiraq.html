Quentino Qiraq

Alphabet Hero

Quentin Fruit (Artificial Power Fruit)

Quentino Qiraq is a Power Fruit researcher, who ate the first artificial fruit ever created by human hands, the Quentin Fruit. The fruit, however, grants him the power to infect people with a viral infection, dubbed the "Quentin Virus". This power immediately brought chaos to the Earthworld, due to its quick spread and difficulty in creating a cure to combat it. The chaos grew so much that The Creator were forced to appear for the first time since the Master Knife incident years prior, and brought Quentino deep underground in a special holding place, not risking his power to infect the Deleted World by deleting him.

Abilities

Quentin Virus

(Passive)

Quentino automatically infects any enemy who attacks him with melee attacks of any type with the debuff 'Quentin Virus', which can stack up to 99 times.

Infect

(No Cooldown)

Quentino approaches an enemy and infect them with the debuff 'Quentin Virus', which can stack up to 99 times.

Innate Abilities

The Quentin Effect

(Hidden, Passive)

When an enemy dies under the effect of 'Quentin Virus' Quentino gains a stack of 'The Quentin Effect', which multiplies his 'Quentin Virus' damage. This buff can stack up to 99 times, and it lasts permanently until weekly reset.
