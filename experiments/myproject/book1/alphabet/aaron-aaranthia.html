Aaron "Aweapron" Aaranthia

Alphabet Hero

Weapon-Weapon Fruit User (Ranged version)

Aaron "Aweapron" Aaranthia is a former EOD specialist that eats the Weapon-Weapon Power Fruit (Ranged version). The fruit allows him to have a weapon out of his stump left feet, and he can turn parts of his body into any kind of weapon, but he always chose to have explosives as his weapons due to his previous work as a soldier.
