Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno

Alphabet Hero

Project X Members (Ice-Fire Rare Clan Descendant)

Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno is a child of the rare Ice Clans and Fire Clans. He was born to a father Brian Bonfire and mother Emily Winters, a runaway from both clans due to their predisposed animosity with each other. As a kid, Frozo is trained in handling both elements by both of his parents, and became one of the first member of Project X.

Abilities

Pyromancer

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno scorches the enemy with fire from his hand. This grants him 1 stack of "Heat".

Cyromancer

(Cooldown: 1 Turns)

Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno freeze the enemy with ice from his hand. This grants him 1 stack of "Freeze".

Infernus

(Cooldown 2 Turns, AoE)

Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno creates a mini eruption of lava that hits enemies. This grant him 2 stacks of "Heat".

Thundra

(Cooldown: 2 Turns, AoE)

Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno creates a localized snowstorm that sends iceblocks towards enemies. This grant him 2 stacks of "Freeze".

Split

(Cooldown: 4 Turns)

Frozo "Frozenfire" Ferno splits into two separate elementals that has their own turns separate from each other.

Innate Abilities

Heat and Freeze

(Passive)
Each stack of "Heat" and "Freeze" buffs Frozo's DMG by 10%. These stacks each last for two turns, and the duration is refreshed when Frozo gained a stack of "Heat" or "Freeze". When five stacks of "Heat" or "Freeze" are accumulated, they're unable to gain more stack of each buffs, and skills of the opposite elements are locked for two turns. Frozo's skills are also buffed depending on the buff stacked whilst he received moderate damage of the related element type for two turns.
  • Five stacks of "Heat":
    • Pyromancer also deals Burn damage over time.
    • Infernus transforms the environment, dealing fire damage to any enemy that attacks Frozo or his team.
    • Frozo turns into a giant fire elemental instead of two different elementals, which can attack twice per turn.
  • Five stacks of "Freeze":
    • Cyromancer also deals extra Ice damage to enemies beside the target.
    • Thundra creates a bigger snowstorm that lasts for 2 turns, continually dealing damage to enemies.
    • Frozo turns into a giant ice elemental instead of two different elementals, which can attack twice per turn.
