O'Neil "O" Scherjarsky

Alphabet Hero

Project X: Invisible Barrier/Telekinetic Potion Inventor, Material Ball Dispenser Inventor

O'Neil "O" Scherjarsky is a chemist, engineer, and inventor affiliated with Project X. He invented The Dispenser, a machinery that can create solid ball material out of anything, including dirt. The machinery has a container included, to input material into, including used ball of material. He also perfected a mixture of Invisible Barrier and Telekinetic potions, which was only powerful enough to levitate the ball material produced by The Dispenser.

Abilities

"O" Punch

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

O'Neil "O" Scherjarsky produces an empty barrier the size of a ball, and bash an enemy with it. This attack can be used indefinitely, and can be used to target the environment to gather 3 ammos.

Ball Punch

(Cooldown: 1 Turn, Requires Ammo)

O'Neil "O" Scherjarsky dispenses a material ball and uses it to bash an enemy. This attack requires ammo, and is unavailable once the ammo depletes. Each ammo has 50% chance to not get used up.
