Rufus the Dog

Alphabet Hero

Project X: Mutant Dog

Ronald "Rufus the Dog" R. is a member of Project X that has the ability to mutate into a dog, using his superior speed and smell to great use.

Abilities

Ronald R.

(Toggle 1 Turn, Default State)

Ronald R. turns into his human form, with limited dog features, but gain the benefits of his Humanoid skills.

Rufus the Dog

(Toggle 1 Turn)

Ronald R. turns into a full mutant dog Rufus the Dog, gaining the full benefits of his Dog skills.

Abilities (Humanoid Mode)

Four Hand Stance

(Cooldown: 3 Turn Aura)

Ronald R. stands like a dog, gaining awareness of enemy's attacks and gains 50% dodge chance on all attack for 3 turns.

Nails Slash

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Ronald R. strikes an enemy with his elongated fingernails, dealing damage and small chance of inflicting Infected Wound debuff.

Bite

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Ronald R. bites an enemy, dealing damage and a chance of inflicting Infected Wound debuff.

Rabid Attack

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

Ronald R. lets loose of his inner dog and rabidly attack enemies, dealing damage and small chance of inflicting Infected Wound debuff.

Abilities (Rufus Mode)

Two Feet Stance

(Cooldown: 3 Turn, Aura)

Rufus the Dog stands like a human, giving him more reach and more potential to deal more damage. Buff ATK of the whole party by 150%.

Paw Punch

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Rufus the Dog punches an enemy with his strengthened paw, dazing them. 50% chance to daze an enemy

Bite

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

Rufus the Dog bites an enemy as a dog, dealing massive damage and a chance to inflict Rabies Wound debuff. 50% chance to inflict debuff.

"Whole"some Bark

(Cooldown: 4 Turns)

Rufus the Dog gathers his energy into his maw and barked a single, empowered bark, striking fear in the hearts of his enemies and make them unable to attack him properly. 75% to inflict Fear, and 100% chance to be inflicted with "Fear Rufus the Dog", lowering their ATK by 20%.
