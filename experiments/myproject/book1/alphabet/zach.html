Zach

Alphabet Hero

100 Good Swordsmen Member (Giant's Last Sword)

Zach is the last male Giant of the almost extinct Giants tribe that was wiped out during the Battle of Sticktartica. He owned the "Giant's Last" Sword, taken from the ruins of Sticktartica, and he is on a quest to find Nei, the only other giant in the world, and the last female Giant.

Abilities

Giant's Slash

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Zach lays down a massive horizontal slash that hits multiple enemies.

Giant Roar

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Zach emits a thunderous roar of the giants, intimidating multiple enemies.

Club and Blade

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

Zach takes out an extra club and wreaks havoc on enemies, crippling and bleeding enemies.

"For the Giant's Pride!"

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

Zach focus down and attack a single enemy, dealing massive damage.

Innate Abilities

Giant

(Hidden, Passive)

As a Giant, Zach has two tiers of HP, but occupies two slots of the team. Due to his size, he has a relatively high defense, but his speed is also considerably slower, and he cannot benefit from any dodge related buffs.
