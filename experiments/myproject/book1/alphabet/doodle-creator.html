Default Skin

"Doodle Creator"

Alphabet Hero

The Creator's Gifts (Drawing Pen)

"Doodle Creator" is an unknown person that can create anything out of his drawing. The Creator has revealed that the person is gifted by Him through his drawing pen, but the person is currently missing.

Abilities

Beginner-Level Weapons

(No Cooldown)

"Doodle Creator" creates beginner level weapons, usable in the same turn it is created. Due to this, he has no unarmed attack, as his attack rely solely on his creation.

  1. Knuckles
  2. Sword
  3. Arms

Intermediate-Level Weapons

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

"Doodle Creator" creates intermediate level weapons, that needs a turn to be drawn before it can be used.

  1. Double Knuckles
  2. Dual Swords
  3. Arms: Rifle

Elite-Level Weapons

(Cooldown: 2 Turns)

"Doodle Creator" creates Elite level weapons, which needs two turns to be drawn before it can be used.

  1. Air Punch
  2. 100 Good Swords
  3. Arms: Cannons

Master-Level Weapons

(Cooldown: 3 Turns)

"Doodle Creator" expended all of his effort to create a Master level weapons, which needs three turns to be drawn, and can only be used once each per battle.

  1. Punch From Above
  2. 7 Great Swords
    1. "The Infinity"
    2. "The Invisible Twin"
    3. "The World Shredder"
    4. "The Gun Katana"
    5. "10 Tornado Blades"
    6. "The Twin Minnesota"
  3. Arms: 7 Cannons
