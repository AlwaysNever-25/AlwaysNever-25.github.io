Mitch Tavernien

Project X: Alien Meat

Mitch Tavernien is a speedster affiliated with Project X. He obtained his power after eating a meat of an alien race known for their speed.

Prev:

Next:

Abilities

Whirling Punches

(Cooldown: 1 Turn)

Mitch punches an enemy, and then... punches them again, and again, and again until he doesn't feel like it anymore.

Here, There

(Cooldown: 2 Turns, Aura)

Mitch creates twin mirage of himself that buffs attacks and makes him harder to hit.

Farpunch

(Cooldown: 3 Turns, Aura)

Mitch runs far away, becoming invulnerable for a turn but unable to take any actions. Afterwards, he returned and deals massive damage towards an enemy, crippling them.

Tornado

(Cooldown: 4 Turns)

Mitch runs around enemies and create a massive tornado, dealing damage and creating environmental damage that would damage enemies once on their turn.

Innate Abilities

Superhuman Speed

(Hidden, Passive)

As a speedster, Mitch Tavernien gains two turns attacking enemies.
