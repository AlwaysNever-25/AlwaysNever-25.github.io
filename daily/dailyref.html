Contracts

From the official Rules & FAQ document, Contracts Event are defined as an event similar to "Secret Santa", but the gifts are switched to series recommendations.

In this specific event, starting from the Winter season of 2024, they implemented an EXP system, earned through finishing contracts each season, and mostly through a bounty system, which can be ranked from E-Rank to S-Rank.

These EXP system are, personally, just something like a trust point, since bounty are raffled and for some of them (most likely expensive games) you need specific EXP ranks, which can be attained after reaching certain EXPs.
