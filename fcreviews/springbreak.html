Spring Break 2024

So... this is awkward.

Yeah, for this Spring Season this year, I took a break from contracts. It almost felt voluntary to me, but really, it's because I didn't finish one contract, the hardest one out of my Winter 2024 Contract; Darkest Dungeon.

And I don't think I'll ever finish the game.
