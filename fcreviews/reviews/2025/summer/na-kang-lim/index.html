Procrastination, it's always procrastination. Moving on...

A romcom with a meta narrative that riffed off ORV? Literally the dream. I'm a sucker for romcom, as my anilist profile have shown. (As of writing this, I have 271 entries of Romance, and 198 entries of Comedy, that is visible on my anilist profile. I am a BIG sucker for romcom.)

This series then also mixed it up by giving the MC, Na Kang-Lim... loop powers? Is this now a Re:Zero but romance?!

But seriously though, as the series progress, we get to be introduced to the heroines of the webtoon that Kang-Lim read, Escape: Six Wives, starting with his favorite heroine, Yoo Da-Hee. Her being a twist on the good girl archetype is pretty fun to watch, especially Kang-Lim crestfallen response knowing how Da-Hee really is.
