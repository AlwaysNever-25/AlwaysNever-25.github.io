The Price of Magic

A Puella Magi Madoka Magica Review

"...We understood nothing at all. Not what it meant to wish for a miracle... nor its price." - Madoka

Magic is not magic without consequences.

Or rather, the best description of this that I can find is Sanderson's Second Law. Limitations are more interesting that power, and in this series case, magical girls are more interesting when we know what makes them tick, their flaws, and their consequences of becoming magical in the first place.
