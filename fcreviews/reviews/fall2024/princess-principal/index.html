The Princess and the Pickpocket

Okay so... imagine Spy x Family but it features Alya in her high school era, with fellow spies, assassin from Japan... and her secret twin that we never knew about.

I'm not gonna say that describe Princess Principal. For starters, there is no Loid or Yor here, just one chaperone doing an impression of 21 Jump Street. Second of all, this world is really... bleak. It almost felt like it's set on Berlin but it's actually London instead.
